STRANGER THINGS 5

A supernatural sci-fi thriller set in the small town of Hawkins, where secret experiments, alternate dimensions, and extraordinary friendships collide.

About the Series

Stranger Things blends 1980s nostalgia with horror, science fiction, and emotional storytelling. When a young boy mysteriously disappears, a group of kids uncovers government secrets, psychic abilities, and a terrifying parallel world known as the Upside Down.

Main Characters

Eleven

Psychokinetic Hero

A girl with powerful telekinetic abilities who escapes a secret laboratory and becomes the key to fighting threats from the Upside Down.

Mike Wheeler

The Leader

Intelligent and loyal, Mike is the emotional core of the group, driven by friendship, courage, and his bond with Eleven.

Dustin Henderson

The Comic Mind

Known for his humor and curiosity, Dustin brings heart and optimism while often uncovering key scientific clues.

Lucas Sinclair

The Realist

Brave and practical, Lucas questions risks but always stands firm when his friends are in danger.

Will Byers

The Survivor

His disappearance connects him deeply to the Upside Down, making him sensitive to its presence and dangers.

Max Mayfield

The Fighter

Independent and fearless, Max adds strength and emotional depth, confronting trauma while standing up to supernatural evil.

Inspired by Stranger Things — A tribute to friendship, courage, and the unknown.
